Assembly of People of Kazakhstan
On March 1, 1995, the Assembly of the People of Kazakhstan was established on the initiative of Elbasy. It is important to emphasize that the activities of the assembly contribute to the growth of the international authority of the Republic of Kazakhstan as a country that effectively solves the problems of interethnic relations. The contribution of the Assembly is appreciated at the highest level. The seventh UN Secretary-General Kofi ANNAN, who visited Kazakhstan at the time, called it an example of interethnic harmony, stable, sustainable development for other countries of the world. This event was highly appreciated by Pope John Paul II, who visited Kazakhstan in 2001.
Qusihad, 2022
