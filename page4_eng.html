The main book of the Republic of Kazakhstan
On January 28, 1993, the first Constitution of the Republic of Kazakhstan was adopted. It consisted of a preamble, 4 sections, 21 chapters and 131 articles. The Constitution outlines many legal norms adopted since Kazakhstan gained state sovereignty: people's sovereignty, independence of the state, the principle of separation of powers, recognition of the Kazakh language as the state language, recognition of the President as the Head of State, judicial bodies – Supreme, Constitutional and Supreme Arbitration, and more.
Qusihad, 2022
