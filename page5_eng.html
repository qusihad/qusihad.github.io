National currency
On November 15 of the same year, another attribute of independence was introduced into circulation – the national currency. The decision to introduce and issue the national currency as soon as possible was another step towards the economic and financial independence of Kazakhstan. Today, the Kazakhstani tenge is an example of combining traditional culture and modern technologies. By the way, tenge has been awarded the title of the most beautiful currency in the world three times. The first award was awarded in 2012 for a 10,000 tenge banknote. After that, the world banking community also marked banknotes in denominations of 1000 and 5000 tenge.
Qusihad, 2022
