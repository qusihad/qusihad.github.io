Acceptance of independence of the Republic of Kazakhstan
On December 16, 1991, the Constitutional Law "On State Independence of the Republic of Kazakhstan" was adopted by the resolution of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Kazakhstan. This date will forever remain the brightest page in the glorious history of our country. It marked fundamental changes in the fate of the nation, the acquisition of genuine freedom by the people of Kazakhstan, the revival of their dignity and spiritual values. We have been given the opportunity to freely realize our economic, intellectual and cultural potential. Kazakhstan has reached high milestones in many areas.
Qusihad, 2022
